OUR TEAM

Claremont McKenna College's Model United Nations (CMCMUN) is an award-winning and nationally-ranked team with four "Best Small Delegation" awards from the international WorldMUN competition, on top of numerous individual and delegation awards at national MUN conferences. We are a family of 35 students across class years and academic majors. Our team grows closer with each weekly meeting, as we work together to identify and develop each other's strengths and weaknesses.
